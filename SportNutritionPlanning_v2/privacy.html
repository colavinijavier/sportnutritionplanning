Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

SportNutritionPlanning ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This page summarises what we collect and why. For questions, email hello@sportnutritionplanning.app.

What we collect

When you create an account we store: your email address, your nutrition profile (age, sex, height, weight, sport, restrictions, goals) and the meal plans generated for you. Family members you add are stored as profile data linked to your account. If you upgrade to Pro, we store your Stripe customer ID and subscription status; payment card details are handled entirely by Stripe and never touch our servers.

Why we collect it

To generate personalised nutrition plans, scale recipes for your household, persist your settings across devices, and process payments for Pro users. We do not sell your data and we do not run ads.

AI processing

Your profile data is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate meal plans and to respond to AI features. Anthropic does not retain user data for training when called via the API. See Anthropic's privacy policy for details.

Your rights

You can export or delete your data at any time from your account page. Account deletion removes all stored profile, plan and subscription data within 30 days.

Cookies

We use only the cookies strictly necessary to keep you signed in. No tracking, no advertising cookies.
